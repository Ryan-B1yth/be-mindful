Forest

Being out in nature is a natural remedy for stress. Forest areas allow us to reconnect with nature in a direct way, linking us to the roots of the Earth, allowing us to breathe fresh, clean air.

Mountains

Mountains and hills combine connection with fitness. Packing a lunch and hiking with a group is a great way to connect and experience nature with new people.

Beach

Coastal areas are great places for walking and contemplation. Sea water can help heal wounds and the great expanse allows for direct access to the vitamin D supplying sun.
