What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness is about being aware of the present. Self awareness about one's feelings, emotions, and thoughts, and being able to calmly reflect on their meaning and interpretation.

What is meditation?

Meditation is a powerful process by which you can find peace and a sense of calmness whenever you require it. Meditating for as little as 10 minutes can lower your heart rate and blood pressure, reduce fatigue, and improve your mood.

What is yoga?

Relaxing

Yoga is a workout, but it can be taken at your own pace. It allows for a slow build of strength and flexibility whilst massaging your muscles from within.
